🧠 Your opponent: an AI that acts

It's not a chatbot answering questions.

  • It has a personality (driver, enthusiast, empathetic, analytical) and speaks with real voice and emotions: it gets excited, hesitates, gets annoyed.
  • It hides a real objection behind the one it tells you. Just like real leads.
  • It has temperature, trust and patience — and you win or lose them with every line.
  • It can buy from you... or hang up on you.

  • Sell by text or with your voice. The lead talks back, in whatever mood you put them in.
  • At the end: a methodology breakdown, a score, and exactly what to fix next time.

🎬 Mode 2: The team Arena

Four roles. One call. Everyone learns.

🎭 THE LEAD — plays the buyer following the AI's secret script. The better they act, the more the team learns.

🕵️ THE OBSERVER — sees EVERYTHING: the hidden layer, the lead's inner thoughts. Grades live and votes.

🎛️ THE FACILITATOR — the movie director. Pauses, changes stages... and fires surprise events when nobody expects them.

⚡ The 3 rules of the Arena

1. Act for real. If the lead gives the sale away, nobody learns anything.

2. Lose without excuses. Every defeat in here is worth more than ten easy closes out there.

3. Come back tomorrow. Champions don't train once.
