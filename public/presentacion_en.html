🧠 Your rival: an AI that acts

It's not a chatbot that answers questions.

  • It has a personality (driver, enthusiast, empathetic, analytical) and speaks with a real voice and real emotions: it gets excited, hesitates, gets annoyed.
  • It hides a real objection behind the one it tells you. Just like real leads.
  • It has temperature, trust and patience — and you win or lose them with every sentence.
  • It can buy from you... or hang up on you.

🎯 Mode 1: Practice solo

Your private gym. Open 24/7.

  • Pick a level and industry → the AI generates a unique prospect with a story, a problem and a budget.
  • Sell by text or with your voice. The lead talks back, in whatever mood it's in.
  • Train one specific stage (opening, objections, closing) or the full sale.

🎬 Mode 2: The team Arena

Four roles. One call. Everyone learns.

🧑‍💼 THE CLOSER — the protagonist. Sees only what they'd see in real life. Their mission: close.

🎭 THE LEAD — plays the buyer following the AI's secret script. The better they act, the more the team learns.

🕵️ THE OBSERVER — sees EVERYTHING: the hidden layer, the lead's thoughts. Scores live with a rubric and a listening log.

🎛️ THE FACILITATOR — the movie director. Pauses, switches stages... and fires surprise events when nobody expects them.

🩺 At the end, the truth

Every session ends with a methodology analysis:

  • A 5-metric radar: frame control, pain depth, objection isolation, detachment and micro-commitments.
  • What went well, what to improve, and one concrete tip for your next fight.
  • If you closed, the commission in play-dollars hits your account and lifts you up the ranking.

Solo practice earns 0.5× — real fights with your team are worth double.

🏆 Something real is at stake

  • World ranking — every session counts, with your flag next to your name. Let them know who closes.
  • The Scouting pool — trainers scout here. Your fight history is your live CV: real job offers are waiting.
  • VIP Proposals — when you're ready for real leads: proposals with video, open tracking and direct WhatsApp. You know who opened it, and when.

🥊 Your first fight, in 3 steps

1Sign up — email or Google. Pick your flag on your level card.

2In the Lobby tap "Practice solo" — choose industry, level and (optionally) one specific stage.

3When you finish, tap "Analyze" — read your radar, save the tip, come back tomorrow.

From the door to the first round: under 60 seconds.

🎬 The team Arena, in 4 steps

1Create a room from the Lobby and share the code with your team.

2Everyone picks a role: Closer, Lead, Observer or Facilitator.

3The Facilitator generates the scenario and starts the call. The 🌐 Time zones button shows everyone's local time.

4After hanging up: "Analyze & Save" — the commission goes to the Closer and everyone sees the radar.
