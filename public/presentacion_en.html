💡 What is Sales Arena?

It's your professional training ground.

A dynamic designed to simulate real-world sales scenarios.

Golden Rule: Total immersion. It's forbidden to say "in real life I would say...". Just say it directly!

⚙️ Usage Dynamics

  1. 🎬 Preparation: Define scenario and objective.
  2. 🎭 Assignment: Closer, Lead, Observer.
  3. ⚔️ The Arena: Uninterrupted simulation.
  4. 📊 Retrospective: Feedback and areas for improvement.

🖥️ How to use the App

  1. Create Room: The organizer generates an ID in the Lobby and shares it.
  2. Join: Everyone enters the same ID and chooses their role.
  3. Configure: The Facilitator picks the sales scenario.
  4. Simulate: Start the timer (⏱️) and begin the Role-Play.
  5. Vote: When finished, use the feedback panel to evaluate.

🧑‍💼 Role 1: The Closer

The Protagonist

Objective: Guide the conversation towards the close.

👇 (Swipe down)

Closer Interpretation

  • Absolute immersion in the character.
  • Active listening to the client (don't read a script).
  • Handling real objections.
  • Leadership and time management.

🧑‍💻 Role 2: The Lead

The Challenger

Objective: Authentically represent the prospect.

👇 (Swipe down)

Lead Interpretation

  • Adopt an archetype (busy, skeptical...).
  • Realistic toughness: don't give away the sale, but don't be stubborn without reason.
  • Bring day-to-day objections to the table.

Your job is to make them think and work for it.

🕵️‍♂️ Role 3: The Observer

The Strategist

Objective: Provide actionable feedback.

👇 (Swipe down)

Observer Interpretation

  • Silent mode: Do not intervene during the simulation.
  • Take notes on: Tone of voice, posture, quality of questions.
  • Sandwich Technique: Positive Comment -> Area for Improvement -> Positive Comment.

🏆 The 3 Golden Rules

  1. Mistakes are the path: Losing a sale here generates learning.
  2. Absolute seriousness: Nervous laughter breaks the simulation.
  3. Criticize the process, not the person.
